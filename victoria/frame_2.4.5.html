Reassessment 

Your assessment includes:

NatHERS Reassessment
and Report

A NatHERS Reassessment looks at how well your home maintains  a comfortable temperature all year round. The results are given as a star rating from 0 to 10. The higher the rating, the more efficient your home is at staying cool in summer and warm  in winter without cranking up the AC or heating. 

Whole-of-Home
Reassessment

A Whole-of-Home Reassessment checks that the overall energy use of the home meets NCC 2022 requirements, not just the building shell. It includes heating, cooling, hot water, lighting, pool/spa systems, and solar panels to calculate total annual energy use and performance.

Updated NatHERS
Certificate

This is a nationally recognised regulatory and accredited certificate showing your home’s energy star rating out of 10. The CSIRO-developed engine generates the results and forms the basis of the BASIX thermal comfort score.
